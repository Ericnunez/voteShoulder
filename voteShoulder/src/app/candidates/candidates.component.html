Candidate 1 Democrat

The former two-term alderman unsuccessfully challenged Mayor Rahm Emanuel in 2015 and staged a long-shot bid to unseat Cook County Board President Toni Preckwinkle earlier this year. Fioretti was born and raised in Chicago and attended Mendel High School. He has a political science degree from the University of Illinois and a law degree from Northern Illinois University. He is a civil rights lawyer.
